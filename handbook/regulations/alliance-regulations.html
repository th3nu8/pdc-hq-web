6. Alliance Regulations

6.1 Allied Faction Privileges

  • PDC will accepts any allies with a minimum member count of 5 official members.
  • PDC offers VIP protection services/events to tier 3 allied factions.
  • PDC allows allies to host or bring additional personnel from PDC into their own events should they want additional attendance.
  • PDC offers training services to allies if they need additional help hosting training/onboarding events.
  • PDC offers advising to allied faction HICOM.
  • PDC will set up dedicated radio channels for tier 2 allied factions on our Mumble server.
  • Tier 2 allied factions may advertise within PDC or any future PDCJOC channels.

6.2 Alliance Tiers

  • Tier 1: Allied Faction (New to the alliance.)
  • Tier 2: (Has been allied with PDC for awhile.) Trusted Allied Faction
  • Tier 3: (Has been allied with PDC for awhile with extensive history of allied HICOM cooperation with PDC HICOM.)
