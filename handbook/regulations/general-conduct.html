1. General Conduct

1.1 General Conduct Regulations

  • No NSFW content.
  • No hateful/extremist content.
  • No malicious links, scams, or phishing attempts.
  • No spamming.
  • Keep usernames, profile pictures, personal bios professional.
  • No exploiting.
  • No impersonation of PDC personnel. This includes anyone with increased authority within the faction.
  • Just have common sense, and you'll do just fine.
  • No arguing in factional channels. Leave it in DMs.
  • Report issues via ticket tool instead of escalating.
  • Leave personal beliefs/ideals that may lead to arguments out of factional channels. This includes topics such as or similar to:
  • Politics
  • Sexual Orientation Beliefs
  • ETC.
