PDC Rank Structure

PDC General Rank Structure

1. Overview

  • Chain of Command
  • Below is the PDCs chain of command and rank structure. It will show a basic overview of where the rank is placed in terms of authority, who you should listen to if they are above you, etc.
  • PDC CoC Notes
  • You should listen to Command members and above, but they should not scream at you. If they do, report them to a person above them or in serious cases take it to the Chief of Staff, Chief of Internal Affairs, Chairman, or Founder of PDC.

2. Chain of Command

Absolute Authority
Founder & Chairman
Extreme Authority
PMC Commander & Deputy Commander Board of Directors
High Authority
PMC Company CO/XO (2 Platoons) PRD CO/XO PAD CO/XO PBOD CO/XO PESA (Lead Rank) PIEA (Lead Rank)
Moderate Authority
PMC Platoon CO/XO PRD Cadre PAD Cadre PBOD Cadre PESA Agent PIEA Investigator

3. Executive Ranks

1. Founder Unobtainable. Runs all areas of PDC.
2. Chairman Hand-picked by Founder. Assists Founder.
3. Director Hand-picked by Founder/Chairman. Runs an important section of PDC.
4. Assistant Director Hand-picked by Director. Assists their assigned Director.

4. HICOM Ranks

1. Commander Handpicked by Chief of Staff. Ensures daily operation of PDC. Inclduding Patrols being hosted, Operator Trainings, etc.
2. Deputy Commander Handpicked by Commander, COS. Assistss PDC Commander.
3. Colonel Handpicked by Commander, COS. Runs a PDC Division.
4. Major Hand-picked by Colonel. Assists their assigned Colonel.

5. Command Ranks

1. Captain Handpicked by Commander/Deputy Commander The previous, + hosting operations.
2. 1st Lieutenant Handpicked by Platoon CO. The previous, + assisting their CO.
2nd Lieutenant. Earn 30 VPs after becoming an Instructor. Host patrols, gamenights, etc. Platoon
3. Instructor Promoted after training. Tasked with processing new PDC members + hosting cert trainings.
4. Trainee Instructor Selected by Platoon CO. Tasked with completing their Instructor training.

6. NCO Ranks

1. Divisional NCO. Obtain the NCO rank in a division. Instructor ranks in PDC divisions. Assist in running their division.
2. Sergeant. Pass the Squad Leader training. Has priority to lead a team in PDC events.

7. Contractor Ranks

1. Divisional Operator Join a division. Tasked with divisional duties.
2. Senior Operator Achieve 75 Valor Points. Tasked with progressing in PDC.
3. Field Operator Achieve 50 Valor Points. Tasked with progressing in PDC.
4. Operator Achieve 25 Valor Points. Tasked with progressing in PDC.
5. Junior Operator Achieve 10 Valor Points. Tasked with progressing in PDC.
6. Probationary Operator Pass the PDC Operator Course. Tasked with progressing in PDC.

5. Trainee Ranks

1. Trainee Pass PDC application. Tasked with completing their training.
