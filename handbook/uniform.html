PDC Contractor Uniform & Gear Guide

Apparel

  • Top: G4 Combat Shirt (Khaki or Ranger Green), Any Civil H000 colored shirt.
  • Bottoms: G3 All Weather Field Pant (Varicam)

Accessories

  • Facewear: Ski Mask, any Balaclava, any Gaiter. (Optional)
  • Earwear: Any make it match.
  • Handwear: Slingshot Tactile (Black or Coyote)
  • Wristwear: Any make it match.
  • Footwear: Generic Tan.
  • Eyewear: Any but aviators or 6B50.

Headgear

  • Knit Cap (Black)
  • Mesh Cap (Black, Backwards or Forward)
  • Ball Cap(Black, Backwards or Forwards), no patch.
  • Gator Bump (Tan) (Mandatory for Night-Ops)

Headgear Customization

  • Must haves (if Gator Bump):
  • Night Vision Shroud accessory: L4 G24 (No nightvision unless told to use for event.)
  • Loopface Accessory Rear Bottom: Counterweight (Tan)
  • Loopface Accessory Rear Middle: HALBOC 640-01
  • Loopface Accessory Rear Left Side: O Mark
  • Loopface Accessory Rear Right Side: O Mark
  • Customize everything else to your liking.

Chest Rig

  • MPC 2.0, custom is fine too. (Varicam)

Chest Rig Customization

  • Must haves:
  • Mags on the front configured to running your preferred mags.
  • Either smokes or flashbangs on the left or right side.
  • A back panel, any just make it match.
  • Everything else customized to your liking. Make it make sense, make it match.
  • (Only restrictions):
  • No antenna, that’s for radio operators.
  • Must be rifleman mags on your rig, or pistol.

Belt

  • Taskforce belt. (Varicam)

Belt Customization

  • Customize it to your liking, make it make sense, make it match.

Customized Reference

  • Essentially, just add some stuff like radios, maybe extra mags here and there, some pouches including medical ones, glowsticks etc. Be unique, just make sure it’s a rifleman kit until you get your certifications for other classes.

Breacher Gear

  • Just swap out your rifle mags for SMG or Shotgun mags depending on which you’re running for your breacher slot.

Radio Operator Gear

  • Add a radio pouch on your back on either side of your back panel and make sure you have a long antenna on the radio you add into the pouch. Any works.

Marksman Gear

  • Just swap out your mags for the appropriate mags corresponding to what you’re using.
  • May also use the boonie cap.

MG Gear

  • Just add box mags somewhere on your kit.

Grenadier Gear

  • Add 40MM nade pouches somewhere.

Drone Operator Gear

  • Same thing as the Radio Operator basically.
