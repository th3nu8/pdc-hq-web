Overview

Internal Affairs is responsible for maintaining professionalism, discipline, and accountability throughout the regiment. The division conducts investigations into misconduct, policy violations, abuse of authority, and any actions that may negatively impact the unit. IA operates independently to ensure all investigations are handled fairly and without bias.

Our Promise

All reports submitted to IA are reviewed thoroughly. Personnel found in violation of regulations may face disciplinary action depending on the severity of the offense.

Responsibilities

  • Investigating misconduct reports
  • Reviewing evidence and witness statements
  • Conducting internal inquiries
  • Enforcing unit regulations
  • Maintaining professional standards
  • Recommending disciplinary action

Authority

  • Access to investigation materials
  • Authority to interview personnel
  • Ability to open formal investigations
  • Direct reporting to High Command
