Overview

The Aviation Division is the backbone of our faction's airspace dominance. Our mission is simple: own the skies, support the ground, and dictate the tempo of the battlefield. From high-altitude air superiority to low-level combat insertions, if it flies, we operate it, manage it, and master it.

Primary Responsibilities

  • Air Operations Management: Controlling the airspace, managing flight lines, and coordinating active air assets during operations.
  • Close Air Support (CAS): Delivering precise, devastating ordnance to eliminate high-value targets and relieve ground pressure.
  • Logistics & Transport: Executing high-speed combat insertions, extractions, and resupply drops.
