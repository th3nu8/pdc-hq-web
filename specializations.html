About PDC

The Praetorian Defense Corps is a premier milsim organization dedicated to tactical excellence and realistic military operations in Blackhawk Rescue Mission 5. We emphasize discipline, teamwork, and professional conduct in all our operations.

Whether conducting executive protection, covert raids, or forward reconnaissance, every operator is expected to uphold the standards that define PDC. We train for realistic scenarios and expect professional conduct on and off the battlefield.

As a PMC faction, we provide security and tactical support across multiple environments — serving our own members and allied factions with the same level of commitment and precision.

What We Offer

  • Structured rank progression and operator certifications
  • Weekly operations across five specialized divisions
  • Realistic loadouts, uniforms, and radio procedures
  • Active community with handbook resources and training
